Welcome to Bagpipe Symphony

 

 

This is the website of Bagpipe Symphony, a company created in 1957 by the musician James Falcon in Scotland. Nowadays it is one of the biggest companies in the manufacturing and reparation of handmade bagpipes on the world.

 

In this website you can find information about our products and services, and you also can read about our history and the lastest news about bagpipes.

 

 

 

 

Bagpipes are a wind instrument using enclosed reeds fed from a constant reservoir of air in the form of a bag. Though the Scottish Great Highland bagpipes are the best known in the Anglophone world, bagpipes have been played for a millennium or more throughout large parts of Europe, northern Africa, and western Asia, including Turkey, the Caucasus, and around the Persian Gulf. The term "bagpipe" is equally correct in the singular or plural, although in the English language, pipers usually refer to the bagpipes as "the pipes", "a set of pipes" or "a stand of pipes."
