About Us

 

 

Product warranty

We are confident in the great quality and robustness of our bagpipes. That is why all our products have a 5 years warranty. If you bagpipes get deteriorated in the first 5 years, we offer free repair. You can contact us for more information of the warranty process and conditions.

 

Repair

We offer repair services for any bagpipe. You can trust our craftsmen to fix and make the best of you bagpipe. You can contact us for more information about the repair process.

 

Visit of our factory

We want to share our craft and handwork methods to everyone. That is why we organize visits of our factory every first Wednesday of each month. The visit is free and open to anyone. You can find our exact location in the contact section.
